## Research Methodology

### How Sources Were Selected

1. **Relevance** - Direct applicability to AI-assisted development
2. **Recency** - Published 2023-2025 (rapidly evolving field)
3. **Authority** - Peer-reviewed or recognized practitioners
4. **Actionability** - Provides implementable guidance

### How Sources Are Used

- **Principles** synthesize multiple sources
- **Rationale** references specific findings
- **Implications** derive from practical experience
- **Maturity levels** align with adoption research
