## About This Blog

The LocalM™ AiD Blog publishes in-depth research and analysis on the intersection of enterprise architecture principles and AI-assisted software development. Our articles are designed for:

- **Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs)** evaluating AI coding tool risks
- **Chief Technology Officers (CTOs)** building AI governance strategies
- **Enterprise Architects** designing frameworks for AI tool adoption
- **Compliance Officers** navigating regulatory requirements
- **Development Leaders** implementing secure AI-assisted workflows

All articles cite reputable sources including peer-reviewed research, official regulatory guidance, and enterprise reports from organizations such as **McKinsey**, **Gartner**, **PwC**, **Deloitte**, **NIST**, **OWASP**, and the **PCI Security Standards Council**.
